## How It Works

### Values Pillars (Module 11)
- **Purpose**: Organize discussions by core values
- **Outcome**: 49 seed questions resolved by community consensus
- **UI**: Space constellation map showing progress

### Neural Pollinator (Module 12)
- **Purpose**: Thought Chambers, Seeds, and Pods
- **Relationship**: Chambers organized BY pillars (Freedom Chamber, Justice Chamber, etc.)
- **Integration**: Natural connection - pillars are the structure, chambers are the rooms

### Keystone Timeline (Module 13)
- **Purpose**: 7-year governance journey tracking
- **Relationship**: Pillar completion unlocks timeline milestones
- **Integration**: As pillars progress, governance evolves
